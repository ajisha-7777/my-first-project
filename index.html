Lesson 2: Verb Tenses

Let's learn about verb tenses and how they are used in English sentences.

Present Simple Tense

Example: I eat apples every day.

Use the present simple tense to talk about things that happen regularly or facts.

Past Simple Tense

Example: I ate an apple yesterday.

Use the past simple tense to talk about actions that happened in the past.

Future Simple Tense

Example: I will eat an apple tomorrow.

Use the future simple tense to talk about things that will happen in the future.
